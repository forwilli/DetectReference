About SwanRef

With the rise of AI writing tools like ChatGPT, academic integrity faces a new challenge: AI-generated citations that don't exist.

The Problem We Solve

Studies show that up to 15% of citations generated by AI language models are completely fabricated. These "hallucinated" references can seriously damage academic credibility.

Our Solution

SwanRef instantly verifies academic references by checking them against:

  • CrossRef database (150+ million papers)
  • Google Scholar
  • Publisher databases

Free Forever

We believe academic integrity tools should be accessible to all students and researchers. SwanRef will always be free.
